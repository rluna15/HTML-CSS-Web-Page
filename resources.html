Learning 2D

For those of you that are interested in 2D art. Here are a couple of sourcers to help you get satarderd

Scott Robertson
Scott Robertson is an industrial designer that is well know for his transportation design. Although hisss main focus is transportation design, all of the skills he show in his two books can be applied to video game design.
Feng Zhu School of Design
This is a school that was started up by concept artist Feng Zhu. The school offers a one year program that will take from a complete noob to a professional in no time. At the end of the course the students participate in an exebition. Where various reps from studios go and offer the students an opportunity to work with them.
James Gurney
He's a traditional artist that focuses in illustration. His books are all about color theory and lighting for story telling.

Learning 3D

If 2D is not to your liking. Here is a list of sources to get you starded on 3d.

CG Cookie
A place to learn 3D modeling for beginners.
Cube Brush
It's a market place for other artists to sell courses, models etc. The founder, Marc Brunet has released a couple of courses for 3D as well as courses for 2D artists.
